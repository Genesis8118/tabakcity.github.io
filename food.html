Welcome to Tabaco City, a vibrant destination in the heart of the Philippines where culinary delights await! Nestled between scenic landscapes and rich cultural heritage, Tabaco City offers a diverse array of dining experiences to satisfy every palate. From bustling street food stalls offering local delicacies to cozy cafes serving up international flavors, this city is a haven for food enthusiasts.

Tabaco City Food Bazaar

A vibrant and exciting event that showcases the diverse culinary talents and flavors of Tabaqueno, a community known for its rich cultural heritage. The food bazaar takes place three times a year, coinciding with the celebrations of Women's Month, Tabak Festival, and the Christmas Season.

Tabaco City Food Park

The Tabaco Foodpark, conveniently located next to the busy Tabaco City Public Market in Divino Rostro, Tabaco City, is a colorful culinary jewel offering a unique street food experience. This open-air delicious foods offers a broad selection of Filipino street delicacies, including famous fishballs, crispy chicken skins, and the renowned kwek-kwek (quail eggs in orange batter). Its strategic position provides a dynamic backdrop for guests to immerse themselves in the bustling environment of local street food culture. As the sun goes down, the food park changes into a bustling social center where visitors can eat delectable snacks, swap tales, and absorb the city's dynamic vitality.

Ishi Aya Garden Bistro

Ishi Aya's Garden Bistro, located in Tabaco City, has quickly become one of Albay's fastest-growing culinary attractions, garnering praise on local and national television as well as social media platforms. This scrumptious must-try experience is inspired by the owner's globe travels and features a combination of tastes from Malaysia, Indonesia, Japan, and the Philippines. The menu reflects the owner's global palette, with a varied selection of foods cooked using skills mastered at prominent culinary schools. Each plate is a work of art, with herbs, spices, and unique plating methods expertly combined to create an amazing eating experience. Aside from the gastronomic delights, IshiAya's Garden Bistro imbues each dish with "pagkamoot" – a touch of love and passion.

Chef Ian's Cafe

A trip to Tabaco Albay in Bicol provides a lovely opportunity to sample the unique delicacies at Chef IAN's Café. This family-run institution in the highland barrio of barrio Bantayan has a friendly appeal, offering a broad cuisine that caters to both vegans and non-vegans. Because of the café's dedication to personalization, each visitor's culinary preferences are fulfilled with a smile, leaving no one hungry. The ability to schedule in advance and pre-order meals adds a convenience touch for those travelling in groups, providing for a smooth and personalised eating experience.

Cafe Molave

Cafe Molave, located in the center of Tabaco City on Zone 2, Tagas Ziga Avenue, encourages tourists to partake in a trip with the right combination of tastes and cheesy delight. This antique comfort welcomes guests to experience culinary delicacies like as ribeye, sausages, and schnitzels from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. To guarantee a smooth experience, the café invites visitors to make reservations ahead of time and to pre-order their favorite foods. While Cafe Molave's culinary skills are impressive, coffee lovers may prefer alternative beverage options. Despite this, the cafe's dedication to create a welcoming atmosphere makes it a must-see in Tabaco City.

Lola's Bistro

Discover the culinary richness of Matagbac, Tabaco City, at Lola's Bistro, situated along Karañgahan Blvd. This modest but packed restaurant shows passion and dedication, offering a unique dining experience in the centre of Tabaco City. Lola's Bistro, a charming hideaway for Authentic Filipino Cuisine, encourages guests to enjoy in authentic tastes that reflect the Philippines' culinary history. Lola's Bistro offers a personal touch as a small business-owned company, offering an intimate ambiance that connects with warmth and sincerity. Lola's Bistro is a charming culinary sanctuary that captures the spirit of Tabaco City's rich gastronomic tapestry for those looking for more than simply a meal.
